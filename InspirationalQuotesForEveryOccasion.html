Inspirational Quotes For Every Occasion
反鸡汤负能量让你精神一整天

Have you met anyone living a life of defeat? You have made a decision which determines the way you live now on the margin. Thinking there is a better life out there without taking action doesn't cut it for success. Make the move. No one is content living mediocre life. No one can stop you. If anyone can do it, you can. Go right ahead, make that move now, inspire yourself!
不负责生产只是大自然的语录搬运家

“I can’t change the direction of the wind, but I can adjust my sails to always reach my destination.”－Jimmy Dean
注重细节，从小事做起，因为你也做不了什么大事。

“Perfection is not attainable, but if we chase perfection we can catch excellence.”
生活不止眼前的苟且，还有读不懂的诗和到不了的远方。

“Try to be a rainbow in someone’s cloud,” Maya Angelou said.
你我本无缘，全靠我花钱。

“Nothing is impossible; the word itself says ‘I’m possible’!” Audrey Hepburn observed.
老说你找不到理想的另一半，难道说你就成了理想中的自己？

“Put your heart, mind, and soul into even your smallest acts. This is the secret of success,”Swami Sivananda said.
人一旦矫情起来，会觉得所有的歌都是唱给自己听的。

“It is during our darkest moments that we must focus to see the light,” Aristotle Onassissaid.
要记住那些陪你聊天到深夜的人，就是因为他们，才让你的黑眼圈那么重，皮肤那么差。

“Change your thoughts and you change your world.” Norman Vincent Peale said.
我们都擅长口是心非，却渴望对方明察秋毫。

“There are two ways of spreading light: to be the candle or the mirror that reflects it,” Edith Wharton said.
暖男燃烧完所有的爱，就成了渣男。爱笑的姑娘总是比别人更容易长鱼尾纹，眼纹，干纹，细纹。

“A hero is someone who has given his or her life to something bigger than oneself.” Are you a hero? Joseph Campbell said.
一个老外边打电话边哭：你根本不爱我，和我在一起只是为了学英语。“你要成为那个人吗？

“No matter what people tell you, words and ideas can change the world,” Robin Williamssaid. Have you been nursing an idea for a project? Try it out today.
就算全世界都反对，你也要坚持自己的梦想，你是谁啊，输到全世界来反对？

“Clouds come floating into my life, no longer to carry rain or usher storm, but to add color to my sunset sky.” How reassuring if you heed the call. remember these words by Rabindranath Tagore.
你只需看着别人精彩，老天对你另有安排。

“Keep your face always toward the sunshine – and shadows will fall behind you.” What shadow is clouding your life now?Walt Whitman remarks.
笑，全世界便与你同笑 哭，你便一个人哭成狗。

“How wonderful it is that nobody need wait a single moment before starting to improve the world.” Anne Frank said.
人生可以随心所欲，但不能随波逐流。

“Don’t judge each day by the harvest you reap but by the seeds that you plant.” No one knows this better than Robert Louis Stevenson.
口中的道德和原则虽然重要，却总是要给手里的利益让道。

“Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known,” Carl Sagan said.
多照照鏡子，很多事情你就明白原因了。

“Two roads diverged in a wood and I – I took the one less traveled by many, and that has made all the difference.” Which road would you have opted to take? Robert Frost said.
人生就是这样，有欢笑也有泪水。一部分人主要负责欢笑，另一部分人主要负责泪水。

“Your big opportunity may be right where you are now,” Napoleon Hill said.
没钱没事业的人，才有时间去提高自己的人生境界。

“From what we get, we can make a living; what we give, however, makes a life,” Arthur Asheconcluded. Give something to someone today. It could be just a smile but give it.
所有抱怨社会不公和制度的人翻译过來只有一句話：请給我金钱，女人和社会地位。

“I believe in living today. Not in yesterday, nor in tomorrow.” What do you believe in?Loretta Young says.
对于今天解决不了的事情，也不必着急，因为明天还是解决不了。

“The measure of who we are is what we do with what we have.”
沒有钱包的充实，哪來內心的宁靜。很多人都本末倒置了。

“Someone is sitting in the shade today because someone planted a tree a long time ago,” Warren said. The benefits of tree planting goes beyond providing shade to climate change. Plant a tree today.
每个人都有幸福的权利，少数人有幸福的能力。

“Memories of our lives, of our works and our deeds will continue in others.”
最丢人的事情是：你恨对方，对方对此毫不在乎。

“I will love the light for it shows me the way, yet I will endure the darkness because it shows me the stars,” Og Mandino wrote in his book, the “Ten Scrolls.”
努力了这么久，但凡有点儿天赋，也该有些成功迹象了。

“Don’t limit yourself. Many people limit themselves to what they think they can do. You can go as far as your mind lets you. What you believe, remember, you can achieve.”
很多人不是心理疾病，是心理残疾治不好的。

“My sun sets to rise again?” Nice food for thought for you.
谁说我不会乐器？我退堂鼓打的可好了
